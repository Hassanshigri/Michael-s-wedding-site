From First Sight to Forever

It all started on a rainy Tuesday at the coffee shop on 5th Avenue. Sarah spilled her latte, Michael offered his napkin, and the rest is history.

What began as a chance encounter blossomed into five beautiful years of adventure, laughter, and endless love. From hiking the Swiss Alps to dancing in Barcelona, we've built a life filled with memories.

Now, we're ready to begin our greatest adventure yet – marriage. We can't wait to celebrate this next chapter surrounded by the people we love most.
